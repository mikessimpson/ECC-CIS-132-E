ABOUT

Greetings & thank you for landing here! Fly-paper is the culmination of a decades long passion for the airline & airport industry. I've collected many paper items from various airlinesI'm a retired airline industry professional. I'm now attempting to produce & direct a personal "second act" through entrepreneurship.

Currently, you'll find my small business on EBay under the name of 'Fly-paper'. I specialize in back-issue aviation themed books & magazines. Ultimately, once the instruction provided by CIS 132 is completed, I will transition some portion of my EBay business to a stand-alone website.


Additionally, a stand-alone, dedicated webpage will serve as an "online resume'" and introduction to my past professional experience in the airline industry.
